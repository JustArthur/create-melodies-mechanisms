**Create: Melodies & Mechanisms** brings an industrial twist to music creation in Minecraft.  
Craft your favorite **music discs** from scratch using automated **Create machines**, and experience the magic of music through mechanics.

Forget dungeon RNG — now you **engineer your soundtracks**.

# 🎛️ Crafting the Blank Disc Plate

Everything starts with the **Deepslate Disc Plate** — a crafted blank used to record a music disc.

Use a combination of:
- **Pressing** to mold deepslate into a clean, flat disc.

> Every disc uses the same base, but the ingredients and sequence afterward will define the sound.

# 🎶 Sequenced Disc Creation

Once your plate is ready, it's time to **program the melody**.

- Use **Deployers** to embed unique materials (Emeralds, Echo Shards, Amethysts…) into the plate — simulating the music's "soul".
- **Press** the plate to finalize the recording.

> Different materials lead to different discs. For example:
> - Amethyst Shard + Emerald = `music_disc_otherside`
> - Echo Shard + Sculk = `music_disc_5`
> - And more to discover.

# ⚙️ Fully Automatable, Just Like Create Should Be

Every process is **loopable, automatable, and expandable** using belts, arms, sequencers, and more.  
You control the factory, the rhythm, and the output. No more praying to loot tables.

# ✅ Result

The end product is a **genuine vanilla music disc**, earned through a satisfying mechanical process.  
Perfect for immersive survival bases, Create-centered modpacks, or custom maps where music plays a role.
